Interviews and Media Coverage

Selected Coverage

SPICE (eplus) · 2016

Takashi Yamanaka x Shinichiro Hayashi Interview on "PORTAL"

An interview on the urban myth play "PORTAL," written by Shinichiro Hayashi and directed by Yukichi Matsumoto, where actor Takashi Yamanaka and Hayashi discuss the city as though layering one map over another.

AI HALL · 2016

Interview with Shinichiro Hayashi on "Hyakushiki Succession"

An extended conversation with the director of AI HALL about a new work that reimagines Tennoji Park's "blue-sky karaoke" culture as a contemporary King Lear, where craft, fieldwork, and reflections on Osaka intersect.

AI HALL · 2015

Shinichiro Hayashi x Makoto Sato Interview on the revival of "Times"

Ahead of the revival of the OMS Playwriting Prize Special Award-winning "Times" directed by Makoto Sato, playwright and director discuss the meanings of coin parking lots as sites shaped by time and urban experience.
